i SHOULD like it. theoretically i should like it. aesthetically it's my favorite guys (harada and tenpei sato) plus the director of disgaea 7 (which i loved). but i don't fucking care about action games!!!!!! i don't want to run around hacking and slashing or whatever!!! i put up with it in hundred knight because i love metallia and because the aesthetic was so unique, but this game looks like every other 3d anime world game, i'm bored!!!! i'm so bored, i don't want to look at this!!!!

IN THIS POST I AM A HATER!!!!!!

ok putting aside my silly nitpicks for more serious criticism. NIS is like, desperately trying to move away from disgaea being their only flagship series, that's what they said with phantom brave 2. but even ignoring the world and reused monsters, the systems are disgaea, it's still reincarnation and item world and... it just feels like so much is recycling what was already there to the point it doesnt make any sense? like i wish they would just go all-in and make something brand new??? and they HAVE, they've made all sorts of games--i'm not even against disgaea spinoffs, the prinny games were really great!!! every day i'm praying they make another disgaea vn because i loved infinite so much!!!!!! but there's a fundamental issue, there's two factions of gamers: people who like to hone their skills and reaction time to execute something cool, and people who like to sit there and think and press buttons at their own pace... a puzzle game is always hard sell for action gamers and action games continue to be a hard sell for me. i don't get fulfillment out of the challenges presented by action games, it is always just something i'm putting up with to get to the good parts (usually story...)
