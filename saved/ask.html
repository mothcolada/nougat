Anonymous asked:

What are your honest thoughts about Metallia from The Witch and the Hundred Knight? What do you like most about her, if applicable?

I LOVE METALLIA

GET READY FOR A LONG POST (spoilers for witch100 and labyrinth of refrain)

read more

i want to start by saying witch100 was my favorite game after disgaea 4, it was only soured slightly in retrospect because of refrain, which i also intensely loved but have problems with. i feel like tatsuya izumi hates women, specifically gay disabled women, so he tortures them in all his games for fun. the bastard. but the characters are also very well written and witch100 does at least have a happily ever after BY SOME MIRACLE--i feel like because isara and velnya kissed on screen that's what kept them from getting an ending where they can actually be together and it makes me so mad. metallia was """ambiguously""" gay enough she dodged dying for it, but her and visco still die so much anyway!! omfg

anyway i wanted to add the context of refrain into this becuase i think it's important, that there are a bunch of queer characters in these games WHICH IS GREAT, BUT!!!! he also doesn't fucking get it, as a writer, as a human!!!!! LIKE THE PRINCE IN GALLERIA, AND HOW KITCAT WAS HANDLED, I'M SO--no i can't get into it, it will have to be another post. even lilian was messy, but it was 2013 and i could look past that. because metallia and visco were so good together. i could ignore the harmful tropes cuz it had something i loved, which is girls who happen to fall in love and face immense suffering but somehow overcome it together. that's the good stuff, that's what im here for...

so like, ok, metallia. she's got so many sharp edges. what she does to malia in ch1, that's a lot for a new player to swallow. who could love a character who does horrible despicable things??? (me, it's me)

i think what i've consistently loved most is characters who are treated one way because of who they are on a surface level, but then you ACTUALLY look at them and realize there is so much nuance and they deserve better. metallia grew up completely alone and confined to her swamp for reasons beyond her control. if she tries to leave, she gets sick and dies. the two women who raised her abandoned her for reasons that are incomprehensible to her, and she was left with a boring golem servant and later an evil fairy. she's lonely and resents her position in life so she pours everything into studying magic to have concrete proof of how great she is and why she shouldn't have been abandoned.

she wants desperately to not be alone but she's also BEEN alone for so long she's like, bandaged over this gaping wound pretending like it's not there. she's mean to people and pushes them further away because being vulnerable and honest with them sure didn't keep anyone around. she doesn't trust that anyone WOULD genuinely like her, which fuels the push and pull between her and visco, like she has to put visco in the category of "thing she owns" to justify her desire to keep her around, cuz she can't just say "I LIKE YOU PLEASE STAY WITH ME!!!" because it would be too painful if visco said no!!!!!!!! ough!!!!!!!!!!!!!

their fight in... oh god what was it, i don't have the chapters memorized as well as i do with disgaea lol, at the big event in amataya where she gives visco the potion that's supposed to cure her curse but it fails in front of everyone and visco thinks she just did it to hurt her and metallia barks back because she only knows how to be combative and can't just APOLOGIZE, because clearly something's wrong and her solution should've worked!!! it's so heartwrenching... cuz by that point you know so deeply where each character is coming from and just want everything to be okay again WAHHH!!!!

the whole... dark hero arc..... stitching together all these disperate parallel worlds to try to get HER visco back, meeting torude, the version of her who failed and can only support the metallia that DOES get to be with visco... oh my godddd toruuuddeee........ witch100's really got everything. the line where dulche asks if visco's metallia's lover and metallia's like N...NO/// coming right after the scene where torude's like hey here's a visco, she's real, wanna rub your hands all over her? it lives rent free in my head.

metallia and visco's love story happens so naturally over the course of the whole story, i just, i couldn't even believe it it was so good. when you make it to walpurgis tower or whatever and visco's like so mad at the witches for being mean to metallia thinking metallia has been locked in her room crying about it this whole time, only to get there and metallia shows up giant and crashing the party and visco just laughs like "oh my god of COURSE you were planning this huge elaborate revenge, that's so funny actually???????" top 10 falling in love moments. visco really gets metallia's appeal.

anyway metallia rules... but my favorite character is actually lucchini, if lucchini has only one defender it is me, if lucchini has no defenders i am dead.

FizzyStarBoba asked:

What is your game-making process? Story, character design, coding, etc.

step by step process:

  1. think of a plot and what characters would be needed in the plot
  2. draw concept art
  3. set up the project (usually ren'py) and write the script
  4. draw the character art and bgs as i write so the scene becomes real and so i can code specific faces for specific lines
  5. make songs to go along with the scenes
  6. send WIP builds to friends so they can agree with me about how good it is
  7. repeat steps 4-6 until the game is done

it's pretty much that, except the asset creation part goes in whatever order i feel like.

Anonymous asked:

hello nami! you have inspired me to make my own webcomic! any tips on how to keep a weekly schedule for posting?

aw, yay! what works for me is drawing comics in advance. when i first started apoc, i posted the comics as soon as i finished drawing them... but i went on really long hiatuses because of that, since i didn't really have a good way to pace it out at the time (and i'd get distracted working on games and stuff instead)

when i started planning once-a-week updates, i got a lot more consistent in uploading them without needing breaks. been going strong for a few years now... i usually write down my comic ideas whenever i think of one (i think of them often haha), and if it's a storyline i'll sit down and do a sketch version all at once, then as the next update day approaches i'll make sure i have a comic ready by the day before at least.

you should plan your schedule to be extremely manageable in case life circumstances get in the way, like at one point i went on vacation for two months but because i only update once a week i only had to draw 8 comics in advance. it helps that they're only 3-panel comics too, so they don't take long to finish at all. knowing i can knock one out in an hour or so helps me to not feel overwhelmed when wednesday is looming and i don't have a comic ready yet... i still have to draw next week's too actually..... good luck with your webcomic, anon!
